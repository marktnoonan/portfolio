Bio

I work as a Progam Developer at People Making Progress, managing programs that support adults with developmental disabilities at home and in their jobs. At PMP I also create internal web-based tools for our management team, and public-facing web pages as needed. When I'm not at PMP, I work at Appalseed Productions as an office manager and the web developer for Folkmusic.com.

In 2018 I volunteered to become a co-organizer with Code for Atlanta (a Code for America Bridage), after starting a civic hacking project with them at a hackathon in 2016. I'm also active in Atlanta's freeCodeCamp community and helped introduce and encourage many newer developers to build their skills.

I have a BA in Music and English, and an MA in Ethnomusicology.
